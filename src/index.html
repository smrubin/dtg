The Dump Trump Game
  • Drag Trump to the Dump!
  • Drag the grid to rotate. Move Trump up and down, side to side on the colored lines until he can be dragged to the dump.
  • Gray links stay in place.
  • Blue links move with the grid. Rotate the grid to connect blue and gray links in different ways.
  • Red links pivot with the grid, but point in the same direction.
  • Green links are fixed in place, but rotate with the grid.
